"Ketchup" On Work

I made this web app with three other people at Stony Brook University's SBUHacks 2018 event, using HTML, CSS, and Javascript on the front end and Node.JS, and (almost) the Google Tasks API in the backend. We researched the pomodoro technique used in work and study environments and wanted to put a fun and interactive spin on it that would allow people to use the technique in a smarter way than initially intended. We also won "Best Domain Name" at the event!
