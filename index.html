Brief Bio

I study Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Stony Brook University, and I intend to pursue graduate studies in the future. I really enjoy computer systems/architecture and operating systems. I also enjoy computer science's theoretical aspects, such as the theory behind automata and algorithmic complexity, hence my desire to pursue a double major in Applied Mathematics and Statistics.

I am also an active member of the Spirit of Stony Brook Marching Band, where I am one of the drum line section leaders.

In my spare time, I practice Tae-Kwon-Do, a Korean martial art in which I've received a 4th degree black belt, achieving the title of "Master." I can also be found practicing drums, learning new things in computer science, or making bad puns... REALLY bad puns.
